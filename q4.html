EDward Gaming

About

Edward Gaming (EDG) is a professional esports organization based in China, best known for its competitive presence in multiple titles, including Valorant. The team’s Valorant division was established in 2020, making EDG one of the pioneering organizations in China's Valorant scene. As an all-Chinese roster, EDG quickly gained recognition for its disciplined playstyle, strategic depth, and aggressive execution. Competing in both domestic and international tournaments, the team has played a key role in the development and growth of Valorant esports in the CN region.
